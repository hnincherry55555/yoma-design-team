# UX Copy Voice — Shared Reference

## Defaults (all products)

- Plain language, no jargon. Conclusion first.
- Primary + 1 alternative for every suggested string; flag tone assumptions.
- English first; Myanmar translation happens after English copy is approved (note: MM strings run ~20-30% longer, design for expansion).
- Sentence case for buttons and headings. Verbs on buttons ("Book now", not "Booking").

## Tone by product

| Product | Tone | Watch out |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Car Share | Practical, friendly | Payment/KYC moments: drop playfulness entirely |
| PLUS+ | Professional, efficient | No marketing language in dashboards |
| Better | TODO confirm | |
| Easy Lease | Reassuring, transparent | Rejection/approval copy must be honest and specific; check regulated wording with Legal |

## Error messages

Pattern: what happened + why (if known) + what to do next. Never blame the user. Never dead-end ("Something went wrong" alone fails review).

## Empty states

Pattern: what this area is + how to get value + one clear action.
