> Review cadence: update this file every release. Owner: design team.

## What it is
Self-serve car sharing service in Myanmar. Users find, book, unlock, and return vehicles via the mobile app.

## Key flows
Signup + KYC, search and book, unlock/lock vehicle, trip end and payment, issue reporting.

## Constraints
- Must work on low-end Android devices and unstable network (offline-tolerant patterns)
- KYC and payment steps are regulated: check with Legal/Data Protection before changing data collection
- Bilingual: English and Myanmar (Zawgyi legacy users may exist; confirm with dev)
- Real product references: Grab, GetGo (Singapore), Turo booking patterns

## Tone
Practical, trustworthy, plain language. Booking and payment moments: reassuring, no playful copy.
