# POV & HMW Examples (calibration)

## Good POV
"First-time Car Share users abandoning KYC need to know how long verification takes and what happens next, because interviews showed 6/8 participants quit when the wait felt open-ended."

Why good: specific user, real need, evidence, no solution embedded.

## Bad POV
"Users need a progress bar on the KYC screen." (solution, not need)

## Good HMW spread for the POV above
- HMW make verification wait time feel predictable? (core)
- HMW deliver value to users while they wait? (amplify)
- HMW reduce the number of documents needed at signup? (question assumption)
- HMW make waiting feel like progress? (flip)

## Bad HMW
- "HMW add a progress bar?" (dictates solution)
- "HMW improve the app?" (uselessly broad)
