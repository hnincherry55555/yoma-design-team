# Assumption Map Template

For each assumption, record:

| # | Assumption | Type (user/business/tech) | Importance (H/M/L) | Evidence (strong/weak/none) | Risk rank |
|---|-----------|---------------------------|--------------------|-----------------------------|-----------|

Risk rank = high importance + weak/no evidence first.

## Output section

**Top risky assumptions (max 5):** each becomes a research question in UX 02, phrased as "We believe [X]. We will know we are wrong if [signal]."
