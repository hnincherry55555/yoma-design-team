# Stakeholder Interview Guide Template

**Feature:** … **Product:** … **Stakeholder:** name, role **Duration:** 30 min

## Warmup (5 min)
1. What is your role in relation to this feature?
2. How does this feature connect to your team's goals this quarter?

## Goals and success (10 min)
3. If this ships and works, what changes for the business? For users?
4. How will you personally judge whether it succeeded?
5. What metric moves?

## Constraints and history (10 min)
6. What has been tried before? What happened?
7. What technical, legal, or budget constraints should design know now?
8. What is the hard deadline, and what drives it?

## Risks and alignment (5 min)
9. What worries you most about this project?
10. Who else must agree before this ships?

## Notes rules
Record verbatim quotes where possible. Mark interpretation separately from what was said.
