# Survey Question Bank

Reusable validated formats. Every question must map to a decision.

## Frequency and behavior
- "In the last month, how many times did you [behavior]?" (0 / 1-2 / 3-5 / 6+)
- "Which of these have you done in the last 3 months?" (checklist of behaviors, not intentions)

## Importance / satisfaction (gap analysis)
- "How important is [task]?" (1-5) paired with "How satisfied are you with how you do it today?" (1-5)
- High importance + low satisfaction = opportunity.

## Single-ease question (post-task)
- "Overall, this task was…" (1 very difficult - 7 very easy)

## Pitfalls to avoid
- No double-barreled questions ("fast and reliable")
- No hypotheticals ("would you use…") as evidence of demand
- Translate to Myanmar only after English version is final; back-translate to check meaning
