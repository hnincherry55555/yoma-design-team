## Interview guide structure
1. Warmup: context of their life/work with the product domain (5 min)
2. Main: past behavior stories, not opinions ("Tell me about the last time you…") (20 min)
3. Task-specific probes tied to research questions (10 min)
4. Closing: anything we missed, magic wand question (5 min)

Rules: open questions, no leading, ask for specific past events, follow up with "why" and "show me".

## Consent and data handling
Verbal consent script; anonymize before storage; KYC/financial/location details never leave the research folder. Check with Data Protection lead before external sharing.
