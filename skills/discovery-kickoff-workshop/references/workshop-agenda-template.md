# Kick-off Workshop Agenda Template

**Feature:** … **Product:** … **Attendees:** PM, BA, Scrum Master, Design, (domain owner) **Duration:** 60 min

## 1. Goal alignment (15 min)
Sticky prompt: "In one sentence, what does success look like for this feature?"
Collect one sticky per attendee, cluster, converge on one shared statement.

## 2. Scope in / out (15 min)
Two columns: "In scope" / "Out of scope (this cycle)".
Sticky prompt: "What's one thing people might assume is included that isn't?"

## 3. Success metrics (10 min)
Sticky prompt: "What number will tell us this worked in 30/60/90 days?"

## 4. Open questions and assumptions (20 min)
Sticky prompt: "What are we assuming is true that we haven't verified?"
Capture raw, unscored — scoring happens in the next step (Assumption Mapping).

## Facilitation notes
Timebox strictly. Park unresolved debates in a visible "parking lot" rather than letting them consume the session.
